Privacy Policy

TimeStampify does not store any of your text, files, or personal data. Only the hash (SHA-256) of the content is generated in the browser and timestamped via the backend API.

We don’t use cookies or trackers. Analytics are anonymized using Google Analytics solely to understand basic usage — no personal info is collected.

You are not required to register or log in. This tool respects your privacy — always.
