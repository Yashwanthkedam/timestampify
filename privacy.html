Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TimeStampify (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information on this site.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store your text, files, or any content you submit for timestamping.
  • No accounts or logins are required.
  • We do not use cookies to track personal behavior.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to measure site traffic. This is anonymous and does not identify individuals.

Third-Party Services

We may use Ko-fi to accept user support. Clicking on Ko-fi buttons may take you to their external website.

Data Security

We apply SHA-256 hashing to your input. This is done on-the-fly and not saved on our servers.

Contact

If you have questions, you can open an issue on our GitHub or contact us directly through our Ko-fi page.
