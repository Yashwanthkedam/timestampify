Privacy Policy 🔐

We do not collect, store, or track your content. All timestamps are generated by hashing your input locally and submitting only the digest to our backend.

Your content is never saved, seen, or shared. No tracking scripts, no cookies (except chai), and no nonsense.

Any analytics used are purely for improving the site’s performance — nothing personal is ever collected.
