About TimeStampify

TimeStampify is a free, privacy-first tool to timestamp your content — built for creators, by a creator.

🎯 What It Does

Whether you're a poet, developer, podcaster, or a startup founder drafting ideas, TimeStampify lets you quickly generate cryptographic proof that your content existed at a specific point in time.

🔐 Why It Exists

The internet is fast, chaotic, and often unfair to original creators. TimeStampify exists so that individuals can protect their creative work without legal complexity, signup forms, or expensive tools. Just paste, upload, and download your proof — no strings attached.

👨‍💻 Who Made This

This project was created by a builder who believes in simple, beautiful, and privacy-respecting web tools. It’s currently free and open to feedback, love, and collaboration.
